Key updates to Microsoft

Reward

Starting in July 2025, we will be making updates to the Rewards program.

Here's what you need to know:

  • We're announcing 3 new participation levels with a range of benefits: Member, Silver, and Gold.
  • The daily search point limit will update by level: Member (15), Silver (30), Gold (60). Additional bonus point opportunities will also be available.

As we evaluate the program, we might make more changes to further the benefits. There's no action required on your part.
